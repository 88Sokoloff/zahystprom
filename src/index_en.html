Self-rescuer SHSS-1P KS

Mine isolating self-rescuer SHSS-1P KS - an individual breathing apparatus on chemically bound oxygen with a closed breathing circuit. The self-rescuer has an antistatic housing for use in mines of any hazard category.

Self-rescuer ШСС-1П KS is used to protect the respiratory organs during the evacuation of underground personnel from the atmosphere, unfit for breathing, containing smoke, dangerous gases, and also with insufficient oxygen.

Checking the tightness of the self-rescuer's body is carried out by the regular self-rescue device leak test device, type ASG. Periodicity of inspection according to the Operating Manual.

Advantages of self-rescuer SHSS-1P KS

  • Certified in accordance with the European standard DSTU EN 13794: 2005, which is mandatory in Ukraine
  • A simple intuitive procedure for switching on
  • The instruction for switching on is shown on the housing
  • Automatic starter, triggered when the cover is broken and does not require a manual start
  • Compact size
  • Complete with anti-mist smoke glasses
  • Available training kits for training skills of inclusion and breathing conditions
  • 5 years service life
